<h1>Blue Team & Security Operations</h1>

<p>
  This section documents my focus on defensive security, focusing on the core principles of a Security Operations Center (SOC).
</p>

<h3>Core Domains & Methodologies:</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Log Analysis:</strong> Interpreting system, network, and application logs to identify anomalies.</li>
  <li><strong>Threat Detection & Monitoring:</strong> Utilizing SIEM concepts to detect potential unauthorized activity.</li>
  <li><strong>Incident Response (IR) Fundamentals:</strong> Understanding the lifecycle of an incident from initial detection to containment and recovery.</li>
  <li><strong>Network Traffic Analysis:</strong> Examining packet captures (PCAPs) to identify malicious patterns or exfiltration.</li>
</ul>

<p>
  The objective is to build a robust foundation in <strong>Detect, Analyze, and Respond</strong> the three pillars of effective security operations.
</p>
